About Me

My name is Alanna Cooper. I am a 21-year-old  senior at the University of Miami. I am originally from Closter, New Jersey. I am  majoring in Media Management on the Pre-Law track. I am minoring in both Public Health and Spanish. My goal is to graduate and go to a prestigious law school and pursue a career in corporate or entertainment law.

I like to describe myself as a "renaissance woman" because I have skills in many different areas. If a topic interests me, I want to master it (this drive is probably why I have a double major and double minor in four different subjects and have worked in a dozen different professional fields). It is my belief that you don't have to limit yourself to just one area and, with hard work and commitment, you can excel at almost anything that interests you.

While my ultimate goal is to work in law, I am inspired by everything related to the arts. From writing to design to photography to makeup, I love expressing myself creatively for fun and for the benefit of others. 

GRADUATION

I graduate May 11th, 2019 with a bachelor of science degree in Media Management on the Pre-law track.

FUTURE

After graduation, I will be moving to Paris where I will be getting a masters in law and prepping for the LSAT before returning to  America for law school.
